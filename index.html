🧭 Recommended Workflow

Never trust a single tool. Follow these steps for a reliable integrity check.

📋
1. Paste or upload your text

Use the text area at the top of the page. Paste directly or upload a .txt, .md, .docx, .pdf, or .rtf file. Your text stays entirely in your browser — nothing is sent to any server.

🔎
2. Filter by tier and category

Use the chip filters to narrow the tool grid. Start with Beginners for quick free checks; switch to Professionals for deep academic or code analysis. Filter by category — AI, plagiarism, bibliography, image, etc.

🖱️
3. Click "Check here" on 2–3 detectors

Your text is automatically copied to the clipboard. Each tool opens in a new tab — just paste with Ctrl+V / Cmd+V. Run at least two or three tools before drawing conclusions.

📊
4. Compare scores — don't rely on one flag

False positives are real and common. If two out of three independent detectors agree, that's a meaningful signal. A single flagged result is not proof of anything.

📚
5. Scan citations for hallucinated references

Open the Citation Generator panel inside the text area. Click Scan input text — it finds all DOIs in your text and checks each one against Crossref. Any ❌ result is a potentially invented source.

🔗
6. Generate clean references

Paste any DOI or ISBN into the Citation Generator and hit Fetch to get APA, MLA, Chicago, or BibTeX output instantly. For full reference management, use Zotero (Pro tier).

✅
7. Final integrity check

Use the bibliography tools — Retraction Watch, Scite.ai, Crossref — to confirm that cited papers exist, haven't been retracted, and actually say what you claim. Be honest about any AI assistance.

💡 Tip: use the language toggle (🌐) to switch the interface to Romanian at any time.
